ABOUT US

Who We Are

Leigh Rescue Centre is a family run charity, in and for the community, aiming to give people, ponies and places a second chance.

What we do

We rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome horses and ponies with the help of the community. The ponies in turn - through various programmes - making a difference to the community.

How we do it

  The charity is family run and family funded with the help of valued volunteers and occasional donations. There are no paid staff. Any money is documented and goes 100% on the rescues.


Why we do it

We are passionate about helping people and ponies and see this as our wee, hopefully positive, contribution to the community. 

Rehoming

The rescue normally has a wide selection of horses available for rehoming, ranging from sports horses, show ponies and cobs to foals and yearlings, all at various stages of training. Rehoming is subject to contract and a nominal rehoming fee. A best match sought for each horse based on application forms, references, interviews and visits for safety and welfare reasons.

Volunteering

At the rescue there are always plenty of practical things needing done including stables mucked out, buckets washed, and haynets filled - things most people can do and we love to see people getting involved. There are fences to be erected, DIY to be done and a trailer to be pulled too. For those who are suitably experienced and kind there are also opportunities to tame, train and care for the ponies. It is all about everyone cheerfully pulling together. Unfortunately due to COVID-19 we are presently not allowed to take on new volunteers, but we are happy for you to apply. Hopefully then when everything gets back to normal we can get back in touch with you. All being well, a warm welcome awaits!
