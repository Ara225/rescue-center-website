ABOUT US

Who We Are

Leigh Rescue Centre is a not for profit, family run project, aiming to give people, ponies and places a second chance.

What we do

We rescue, care for and rehome ponies and horses, as well as trying to make a difference in the community around us.

How we do it

We're funded primarily out of our own pockets and most of the work is done by ourselves, though we accept donations and volunteers

Why we do it

We aim to help some of the many neglected and unwanted horses while benefiting the community around us as best we can.

Rehoming

We normally have a wide selection of horses available for rehoming at any one time, ranging from sports horses, cobs to foals and yearling, including those shown here. We do charge a small rehoming fee for each horse, though this very rarely covers the cost of rescuing and looking after the horse.

Volunteering

We're having to put volunteering on hold due to COVID, but we're happy for you to apply for when everything gets back to normal. We normally welcome volunteers from all walks of life. Our setup is still pretty basic and we're definitely nothing fancy, but we'll give you a warm welcome! We need help with a wide range of stuff from shelter building to taming horses.
