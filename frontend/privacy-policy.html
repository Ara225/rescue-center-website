Privacy Policy
Privacy Policy


This page is used to inform website visitors regarding Leigh Rescue Centre's policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information.

If you choose to apply, donate or complete the contact form, then you agree to the collection and use of information in relation with this policy. Additionally, IP and other related data is stored automatically whenever you visit the site. We will not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection and Use

To allow you to apply to rehome or volunteer with us, donate to us or us to reply to your query, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to your name, phone number, and postal address. We store this information under the GPDR Legitimate Interests ground.

We use the information we collect or receive, such as your email address, to communicate directly with you. For instance, this may include communications about your applications, about contact requests, to confirm form submission, and to notify you about horses you might want to rehome based on your application. We may also have to use it to seize a horse that you have rehomed if we have evidence of serious misconduct. Your data is kept within the UK at all times.

Log Data

We want to inform you that whenever you visit our Service, we collect information that your browser sends to us that is called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your computer's Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, browser version, pages of our Service that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, and other statistics.

We use or may use IP address information to: (a) to provide and monitor the effectiveness of our Service; (b) monitor traffic; (c) diagnose or fix technology problems; (d) prevent and/or mitigate DDOS or other abuse of the site

Cookies

Cookies are files with small amount of data that is commonly used an anonymous unique identifier. These are sent to your browser from the website that you visit and are stored on your computer's hard drive.

Our website does not use “cookies” on publicly available site areas, however, some of the third party services we use may.

Service Providers

We may use third-party services for the following reasons:

  • To facilitate our Service
  • To provide a Service on our behalf
  • To protect the Service from abuse
  • To perform Service-related services, or
  • To assist us in analyzing how our Service is used
We want to inform our Service users that these third parties may have limited access to your Personal Information to perform the tasks assigned to them on our behalf. However, they are obligated not to disclose or use the information for any other purpose.

Security

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, thus we strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. This includes following security best practices, using industry standard cloud services, logging access to your data etc. However, no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

In the event that any information under our control is compromised as a result of a breach of security, we will take reasonable steps to investigate the situation and where appropriate, notify those individuals whose information may have been compromised and take other steps, in accordance with any applicable laws and regulations.

Instances where we are required to share your information

We will disclose your information where required to do so by law or subpoena or if we reasonably believe that such action is necessary to (a) comply with the law and the reasonable requests of law enforcement; (b) to enforce our Terms of Use or to protect the security, quality or integrity of our Service; and/or (c) to exercise or protect the rights, property, or personal safety of Leigh Rescue Centre, our volunteers, horses, or others.

Links to Other Sites

Our Service may contain links to other sites. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that site. Note that these external sites are not operated by us. Therefore, we strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of these websites. We have no control over, and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

Your Choices about Your Information

Under the GPDR, you have rights to view the information we have and to ask us to delete it, among other rights. If you wish to excise your rights, please use the Contact Us form. To protect your information, we will ask for verification of your identity.

How long we keep your information:

Log files are normally automatically deleted within 30 days, unless they are required for security purposes. Queries are also automatically deleted within 30 days, unless there is a compelling legal reason. We try to delete unsuccessful rehoming and volunteering applications within 30 days. Successful applicants will usually have their data kept for as long as they have one of our horses or are volunteering with us.

Children's Privacy

Our Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13. In the case we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we will be able to remove it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, we advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately, after they are posted on this page.

Contacting Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
