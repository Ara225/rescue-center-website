HOW IT WORKS

Apply

View the available horses, decide if any seem suitable, and make a rehoming application online.

Discuss

We'll reach out within seven working days to further discuss your requirements. We will also reach out to your vet and/or farrier for a reference.

Meet

If all goes well, we'll invite you to the farm to see the horse you're interested in, and to ensure it's suitable for you.

Finalize

If we're both happy, we'll arrange for the horse you're interested in to be rehomed to you and you'll get to take it home.
