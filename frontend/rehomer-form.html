FAQ

Does filling in this form entitle me to a horse?

No. This is merely the first step in the process; we're just finding out about you so that we know you can care for the horse.

What happens next?

When you submit this form, if you've indicated interest in a specific horse, we'll try to phone and/or email you as soon as possible to discuss and arrange a meeting. Otherwise, we'll try to reach out when we have a suitable horse, though we have no obligation do so.

Who can I contact if I have technical problems with this form?

You can fill in the contact us form or email Anna, the volunteer responsible for this site, directly at anna11199810@hotmail.co.uk

Is my personal data secure?

We make the best possible efforts to secure your data in accordance with the GPDR. It will be sent over HTTPS, stored in a secure cloud database, and encrypted at rest. You can request to have us delete it at any time.

Why do you need this data?

We need to be sure that our horses are going to be safe and happy in their new homes. We believe that the data we request is reasonable in attaining this goal under the GPDR legitimate interests ground. Specifically, it is used for assessing suitability for rehoming, checking up/seizing horse(s) rehomed, contacting for purposes related to rehoming, and/or informing potential rehomers about horses they might be interested in.

Will you send spam or resell my data?

No; we will use the data only for the purposes listed above.

How long will my data be kept for?

It's difficult to give an exact timescale; it really depends on the situation. However, we will review the data we have regularly and delete any unnecessary data. Specifically, if we turn you down for rehoming, we will try to delete your data within a month. You can ask us about the data we have or ask us to remove it at any time via the Contact Us form or authorized email addresses.
